Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?
Simply browse our events catalog, select an event you're interested in, and click "Book Now". You'll be guided through a seamless booking process. Create an account to manage all your bookings.
Can I get a refund on my ticket?
Yes, we offer full refunds up to 48 hours before the event. Partial refunds may be available for cancellations made within 48 hours. Please check the specific event's refund policy.
Are events available in my city?
We host events across major cities in India including Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, and more. Use our location filter to find events near you.
How do I become a speaker or organizer?
Sign up as an "Event Organizer" on our platform. Once verified, you can create and manage your own events, invite speakers, and sell tickets through our marketplace.
Do you offer group discounts?
Absolutely! We offer special pricing for groups of 10 or more. Contact our sales team for custom group packages and corporate event solutions.
